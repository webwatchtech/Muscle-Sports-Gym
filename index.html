Join With Us

Our Class

Our fitness experts can help you discover new training techniques and exercises that offer a dynamic and efficient full-body workout.

Body Building

Sculpt, tone, and strengthen your entire body with Body Building, the original LES MILLS barbell class. This high-energy workout focuses on low weight loads and high repetition movements to burn fat, gain strength, and produce lean muscle conditioning. Join us for the world’s fastest way to get in shape.

Wrestling

Experience the adrenaline rush of wrestling, a high-intensity workout that challenges your strength, agility, and endurance. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced wrestler, this class will push you to new limits and help you achieve your fitness goals.

Cardio

Elevate your heart rate and boost your fitness with our cardio workout classes. From high-intensity interval training (HIIT) to dance cardio, these classes are designed to torch calories, improve your cardiovascular health, and increase your endurance. Join us for a fun and energizing workout that will leave you feeling exhilarated.

Kick boxing

Unleash your inner fighter with kickboxing, a dynamic and empowering workout that combines martial arts techniques with cardio drills. Improve your strength, agility, and coordination while burning calories and relieving stress. Get ready to kick, punch, and sweat your way to a stronger, fitter you.

Body Massage

Experience the ultimate relaxation with our body massage sessions. Designed to relieve stress, reduce muscle tension, and promote overall well-being, this class is perfect for anyone looking to unwind and rejuvenate. Whether you're recovering from an intense workout or simply need a break from daily stress, our expert massage therapists will help you feel revitalized.

Martial Arts

Learn self-defense techniques and improve your overall fitness with martial arts classes. From karate to taekwondo, our experienced instructors will guide you through a variety of martial arts disciplines, helping you build strength, agility, and confidence in a supportive and empowering environment.
